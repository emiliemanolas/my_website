My Passions

Horseriding

I was only 3 months when my mom first put me on her horse. I suppose it was meant to be. Since then there hasn’t be a single year in my life without horseriding.

Every time I saw a horse, I was amazed: moovies, toys and actual horses!

I was always horseriding

I started training when I was only 3 years old and would go to practice lessons once a week, when growing up that frequency increased, I started to pass the “galop” level, participate in competitions and eventually achieved galop 6/7 level.
